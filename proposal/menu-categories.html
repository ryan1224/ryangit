My Pupper

This is my pup. Is he crazy? Yes. Does he have a problem not licking something every living second? Yes, but I still love him so much. Honestly, I don’t think that I could ask for anything else out of a lil pup. He has to chew the squeaker out of every toy he gets, he barks whenever someone isn’t looking at him, and he has the ability to eat an entire baseball cover in about 5 minutes. Most importantly, however, is that he loves me…like, he REALLY loves me. He’s sad when I leave every morning (which sucks) and excited when I come home every day. He makes me laugh when I’m sad and even snugs me at night when I’m cold. I’m so happy you fell into my life and I can’t wait to have more adventures with you in the future buddy.
